Professional Profile

I am a consultant obstetrician and gynaecologist, clinical researcher, and scientific writer with over 15 years of experience in women's health, research, and evidence communication. My work focuses on maternal health, cervical cancer, reproductive health, and healthcare inequalities, with more than 20 peer-reviewed publications, including work published in The Lancet Global Health. I also work with real-world data, policy reporting, and scientific writing, and I write regularly on women's health through Kazona Health.

I trained in clinical research through Harvard Medical School's Global Clinical Scholars Research Training programme and completed executive education in digital transformation and healthcare innovation at Imperial College Business School. I am the author of From Research Idea to Publication: A Practical Guide for Health Professionals.
